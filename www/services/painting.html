About Our Painting Services

At George Jarrell Handyman Services, we offer professional painting services that transform the look and feel of your home. Whether you're looking to refresh a single room or completely change the exterior appearance of your house, our skilled team can deliver beautiful, long-lasting results.

Our painting services include:

  • Interior wall painting
  • Exterior house painting
  • Trim and molding painting
  • Cabinet painting and refinishing
  • Deck and fence staining/painting
  • Wallpaper removal
  • Texture application
  • Color consultation

We use high-quality paints and materials to ensure a smooth, even finish that will stand the test of time. Our meticulous preparation work, including proper cleaning, sanding, and priming, ensures that your new paint job looks great and lasts for years.
