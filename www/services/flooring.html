About Our Flooring Services

At George Jarrell Handyman Services, we specialize in a variety of flooring installations that can dramatically improve the look and function of your home. Whether you're updating a single room or your entire house, we have the expertise to install beautiful, durable flooring that fits your style and budget.

Our flooring services include:

  • Vinyl Flooring: Durable, water-resistant, and available in countless designs and patterns
  • Laminate Flooring: Affordable option that mimics the look of hardwood with improved durability
  • Hardwood Flooring: Classic, timeless option that adds value to your home
  • Subfloor preparation and repair
  • Floor trim and transition installation
  • Old flooring removal and disposal

We handle every aspect of your flooring project with precision and care, from initial measurement and material selection to the final installation. Our team ensures that your new floors are installed properly for maximum durability and a beautiful finished appearance.
