About Our Electrical Services

At George Jarrell Handyman Services, we provide safe, reliable, and code-compliant electrical services for your home. Our experienced team can handle a wide range of electrical projects, from minor repairs to more complex installations.

Our electrical services include:

  • Light fixture installation and replacement
  • Ceiling fan installation
  • Outlet and switch replacement
  • Electrical panel upgrades
  • Circuit installations
  • Troubleshooting electrical issues
  • Smoke detector installation

Safety is our top priority. We ensure that all electrical work is done properly and meets local electrical codes. Our team is knowledgeable about the latest safety standards and best practices in residential electrical work.
